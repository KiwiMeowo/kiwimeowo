Kiwi's Food Log

This is a log which I record the memorable foods I have eaten in the past, because you will never know if one day you can't eat some of your favourite food anymore and it slowly slips out of your memory...


Go to Page:

Clam vermicelli

Taste: ♥♥♥♥♥

Location: 花甲年華 In the Mood for Clam

Price: $65

First taste: Around May 2025

The soup has a mild garlic, cilantro, seafood flavour, but what I enjoy the most is the smell. Maybe because I have had too much strong flavoured food that I feel it's a bit bland, but I'd love it have this once in a while.

HOURAKU Cheese Bouchee

Taste: ♥♥♥♥♥

Location: In most Châteraisé stores

Price: Forgot again

First taste: Around 2025 March

The cake is fluffy, the cream is sweet and savoury with cheese bits, it is very tasty! Make sure to not get it too soggy, or else it won't taste as good.

Pepero almond chocolate sticks

Taste: ♥♥♥♥♥

Location: Convenient stores in Hong Kong

Price: Under $10

First taste: I forgor

I grew to enjoy chocolate with nuts, I enjoy the crunchy texture and the nutty flavour, which compliments the taste of chocolate. With this flavour of Pepero, I get to enjoy this combination often. Might not be a good habit but I sometimes eat this as breakfast when going to school (Kids, eat healthy, don't be like me!!!)

Shrimp sushi with onions

Taste: ♥♥♥♥♥

Location: Sushiro

Price: Like HKD$20-ish?

First taste: Probably 2024

I love shrimp, I love onions, combine the two together and I won't stop eating it. I love how the shrimp is crunchy, and the onions are sweet and aromatic.

Pork Jowl

The flavour is savoury, and the meat melts in your mouth. It is a very good side dish to eat along with noodles.

Maid cafe curry

Taste: ♥♥♥♥♥

Location: Tokyo Akihabara at-home cafe

Price: I forgor

First taste: 21 May 2024

Idk if it's because I tasted bland curry for years in Hong Kong, when I tasted this curry, it feels like the tastiest curry I've ever had. It was so good I temporarily lost social anxiety and told one of the maids that the curry is so good.

Raw oyster

Taste: ♥♥♥♥♥

Location: Tomari Iyumachi

Price: Don't remember

First taste: 1 Jun 2023

Normally, I don't like oysters, the flavour just isn't my taste. And when we got there, my tour mate recommended me to try the raw oysters. I hesitated, but I gave in due to peer pressure lmao. Surprisingly, it tasted so FRESH, it is unlike any other oysters I had ate. It doesn't taste that fishy, but instead it is mildly sweet, just the way I like seafood. Til this day (8 Nov 2024), I still haven't found any oysters taste as good as that.

Mister Donut donuts

Taste: ♥♥♥♥♥

Location: Mister Donut (Japan)

Price: I forgor

First taste: 1 Jun 2023

I was walking around the mall while many stores are starting to close due to typhoon, when I noticed a donut store. There isn't a lot of tasty donuts in Hong Kong (they taste soggy and the chocolate taste like wax 😔), so I never pass up an opportunity to get a tasty donut when I'm overseas. As I was looking at the different flavours, an employee came up to me with a bag of donuts, saying the store is closing, and she gave me (and other customers) the donuts as a gift.

I am especially big fan of the mochi donuts, I like the chewiness. But I think the most memorable part is how I got gifted free donuts, I have never experienced something like this.

Chicken wing ciabatta sandwich

Taste: ♥♥♥♥♥

Location: Near my home

Price: Prob around HKD$50

First taste: 7th grade

It is my sister that recommended me this place. The boneless chicken wing ciabatta sandwich is very delicious, the bread is toasty, the meat is juicy and tender, and as you bite into it, the flavour comes together harmoniously. It is something that helped me got through my parent's divorice (sorry if this make the tune sad), this sandwich always bring me comfort to my stomach.

Sadly the store closed down, and I have never tasted any sandwich as good as this one...

School canteen ham omulet

Taste: ♥♥♥♥♥

Location: My primary school

Price: HKD$20 I believe

First taste: Prob 4th grade

I love buying snacks ever since I was a kid, and I always go to the canteen to check out any tasty food. My favourite from it is the ham omulet. I feel like ham isn't that tasty, but the omulet is so flavourful and juicy, I would lick the bowl clean. I think it's a time I bought three bowls of them because they are so irresistable. I wonder if they still sell these back there...

Crunchy strawberry chocolate

Taste: ♥♥♥♥♥

Location: My local Wellcome supermarket

Price: I forgor

First taste: Don't remember :(

I was craving some chocolate, and randomly bought this chocolate from the supermarket, and HOLY SHIT IT IS SO BANGER!!! What's so special about this chocolate is that the chocolate is made with layers, like croissants (idk how to explain), and between the layers, there's a lot of space. So when you bite into it, it feels like eating crackers, it's so cripsy!!!! I still have no idea why they stopped selling these, they are so fantastic :(((

Tomato Drink

Taste: ♥♥♥♥♥

Location: hana-musubi (Hong Kong)

Price: I forgor

First taste: 2020 I think

Years ago, there's this drink I really enjoy buying along with onigiri. Common tomato juice is very tasty, but this one is very flavourful and it even has tomato bits inside, adding to the texture. It it the kind of drink you would want to have frequently, but for some reason the place stopped selling them? They have corn soup now but it isn't as good as this tomato drink...

7-11 Chicken Breast

Taste: ♥♥♥♥♥

Location: 7-11 (Hong Kong)

Price: HKD$21

First taste: Nov 5 2024

Recently, my sister gave me a bite of chicken breast she bought from the nearby 7-11. I used to think that chicken breasts don't have a good texture on it's own, but shockingly, despite the texture being a bit rough, it tastes quite good! So lately when I'm in a hurry but I'm feeling hungry, I'll go buy one on my way.

The flavour is savoury and kinda juicy, and for some reason I kinda dig the rough texture. The only downside is that there's a bit of a bitter aftertaste I don't know where it comes from.

School canteen chicken fingers

Taste: ♥♥♥♥♥

Location: My old secondary school

Price: Quite cheap, but I forgor

First taste: During secondary school

During secondary school, I went to the canteen a lot, cuz I need snacks to replenish my sanity. There's something called chicken fingers I think (chicken breast cut into slices and fried), and I LOVED them. They are so flavourful and juicy, I would rush down to the playground to get them before they get sold out.

What's more is that if you managed to get the freshest batch, it would be even more juicy and delicious, you wouldn't believe how tasty they are. I wonder if it is reasonable to visit my secondary school just to get a taste of them again...
