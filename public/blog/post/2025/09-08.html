My evil Minecraft ways

I have heard stories of people playing the Sims and doing inhumane stuff to the characters as demonstrated in this video by Matt Rose. In my experience, I often just do normal stuff, the 'worst' I did was marrying a guy just to stay in his house to not spend money on food, also his house is better anyways. Also there's one time I kicked a neighbour's trash can for a quest, I felt so bad I ran away immediately. Seeing how people treat their Sims, I was like "Man I'm sure is tame in terms of gaming..." Then the memories of me playing Minecraft came back.

In the version of Minecraft PE I used to play, there were very limited things to do, far less blocks, no commands, no time changes in creative mode, and you can't really throw items in creative. Still, I found ways to build some amateur but cool buildings, one thing is a conveyor belt using water. While I think it is very cool to move one item from point A to B, I couldn't really throw items from my inventory. An easy way for items to drop is killing animals, if I trap and kill a bunch of mobs in one spot above the water, then they will be moved by the water, transportation!!!

Another thing I made was the animal cooking system. In old PE, you can't access blocks with functions such as chests, crafting table and furnaces, which means I can't cook stuff, which takes away the immersion of play pretend a bit. But eventually, I found out I can get cooked meat by setting the mobs on fire. At that time, flint and steel wasn't a thing in creative (I think), so I had to rely on lava. To avoid the meat getting burnt away in lava, I found out that I can make the animals fall from a high spot, make them pass through a layer of lava, and make them fall to death to safely get the cooked meat. Using the water conveyor belt just now, I can spawn a bunch of animals into the water stream, make them fall through the lava and onto the ground, and finally get lots of delicious meat on the ground. When I pick them up, it looks like I'm eating them, and the popping sound of item picking sfx is satisfying! Yum!

The mechanism above is somewhat forgivable since auto meat farm is a thing, though others make it for survival while I do it for fun. However, the next thing is quite worse.

With me obtaining Minecraft: Java Edition, I now have my hands on redstone. My redstone stuff is rather flimsy, but they do their jobs, and that's what matters. I mainly used them for auto lights and piston doors, but the most morbid thing I built was the hydraulic press. It consists of a 4x4 box and various pistons, a set of pistons on top that acts as a lid, and pistons on the both sides of the box. Why did I make this? I don't know, I just wanted to squish some mobs I guess. I would lure some mobs (or just spawn them) into the box, then I would close the lid so that they won't jump out, and I would activate the mechanism, and watch them slowly or quickly squish to death and have items spray out.

Back then, there were websites that give out free Minecraft accounts, I have no idea where they obtained the accounts, most likely hacking maybe. Anyways I got one account from there at some point, because ,first, I really wanted a player to visit my world, even if that player is another me. And second, I wanted to put the player into the mechanism for the lols. So I squished them multiple times until I get bored. I also squished myself to death multiple times as well. Man I sure was bored.

Minecraft is a sandbox game that allows players to freely express their creativity, even if said creativity is a torture device that doesn't do anything meaningful. I should play Minecraft more, maybe even with my sister.
