I was an Ipad kid, I spent a lot of time playing random games. And as the years passed, many games faded from the App Store, as the developers stopped updating them. There's a lot of games I still remember though, it would be nice to play them again, though I don't think it would be possible...

While the games doesn't show up in App Store anymore, it is easier to find them using "before: [date]" in Google, it filters out anything after said date. I just searched games before 2010, it's over a decade, but I'm certain most of my child games are before that period. And hey, I have gotten some results!

Majority of these games aren't availible anymore, so I just review them based off my nostalgia-coated memories, I don't think I'd enjoy them very much if I play them today.

Spellcraft: School of Magic

One of my favourite childhood games. As the title suggests, it is a game about magic, and you can brew magic spells as cards, kill monsters in dungeons, plant stuff and keep pets (familiars I think). Child me isn't very bright, it did take me a while to understand the attack system, but each card (spell) has different functions, and there are some cards that are only useful to certain enemies (like there are cards targeting rats).

It is quite fun trying to figure out the system, and what excited me the most about this game was the pet system, I mean who doesn't like pets? Sadly, I had to delete the game because everytime I opened the game, it just freezes, and I didn't have anything to fix it.

Flying hamster

Oh hey! A game that is still availible on App Store! This is a game where you control a flying hamster fighting against giant creatures. To summarise the premise, the protag and a girl hamster were playing on the beach, when they found a golden sunflower seed. However, the girl hamster got captured quickly after that, so the protagonist rushes to rescue. And everytime they nearly reunited after each boss fight, she always gets captured again by the next boss Spoilers for the ending: When the two hamsters finally reunited, it turns out the protagonist isn't trying to save his lover, but the golden sunflower seed. As the protag happily enjoys the tasty golden seed, the girl hamster angrily gives him a jump kick. Funny game lmao

Despite how charming the art and story is, the game is incredibly difficult. If you want to beat the game, you have to beat each level consecutively. If you die, you have to start again. You can imagine how tough it was for me, my clumsy little fingers wasn't very good at moving very well. Somehow, I think I beat the game at least once, I mean what other reason can it be that I have the memory of the ending?

Cat Flight (Placeholder name until I find the actual name I SWEAR it's called this)

The gameplay is simple, you throw the paper plane as far as you can, there are also boosts than can help you fly further. The story is quite sweet as well, the protagonist was trying to find a herb to save another cat who's sick, so it's travelling afar using the paper plane.
