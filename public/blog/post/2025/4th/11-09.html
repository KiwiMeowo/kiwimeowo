1. Flower of Love Nigiri

Have you ever seen this type of sushi? This is Flower of Love Nigiri, a popular sushi variety in Hong Kong. For many years, I assumed it originated from Japan, but apparently it is designed by a Hong Kong sushi chef!

This sushi exists due to the chef wanting to mend his relationship with his wife, unable to buy 100 rose, he instead designed this sushi (which looks like a rose) and made 100 of them. While they broke up anyways, he sold them to his store, and received a lot of good feedback.

2. Am I wasting my life?

Recently, Kurzgesagt released a new video about quitting weed. While I never touched weed or abused any drugs, the part about wasting your life away instead of doing meaningful things sounds quite scary, it's basically just depression. I started to think back in my life and panicking, as a socially-anxious person, I don't go out frequently and meet friends, and I often feel ashamed about not partying and socializing all the time.

But after thinking more than 5 seconds, I realised I don't need to have an explosively social life to have a good life. I have written a list of stuff that I feel proud about, I decided to keep track of them lest I feel anxious about not being as "sucessful" as a party animal (I'm not saying the video misleads people into thinking only a social life is a successful one, I'm just prone to feeling shame about being introverted).

3. The only Youtube Sponsor segment I'd watch

I really hate how ads are in literally everything, websites, before videos, after videos, between videos, and SOMETIMES ADDED BY THE CREATORS THEMSELVES. I always skip them before the speaker even starts rambling about the product they totally enjoyed.

However, there is one instance that I would watch through them, which is when the sponsor product matches the video theme. I have been watching the videos by Rachel and Jun, and I always enjoyed the snack box segments, their reactions don't feel forced as they do enjoy Japanese snacks, and a lot of their videos revolve around food as well. I still won't buy the products though lol.
