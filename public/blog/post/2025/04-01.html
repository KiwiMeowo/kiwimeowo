又到愚人節，本來我打算預備啲嘢準備，但係我學業忙到唔記得咗。我可以保證我今日係畫緊嘢嘅。打倉頡好麻煩

幾日前，我有個網上面試，有個網站錄影我講啲普通面試問題。我當時估計個面試會用英文，所以都唔係太擔心。但我發現個自我介紹係要用中文嘅，嚇死我。雖然個面試都算順利過咗，但啲面試員有機會覺得我明明係香港人，廣東話好似都講得好唔流暢 XD。愚人節打廣東話啲blog唔知夠唔夠好笑，但我覺得都係個好玩嘅挑戰。

講番愚人節，唔知大家有冇真係慶祝過呢個節日呢？因為我細到大只聽過愚人節嘅存在，但冇玩過。可能係國外會玩得大啲，但喺香港比較少人喺網上以外會理呢日。我係網上世界裡面，我好多係見到啲Youtuber或其他名人扮退出網圈，然後喺影片尾或其他地方宣布愚人節快樂。睇到第一第二次都算幾好笑，但如果每一個人都咁做，甚至年年都咁玩，總有一日啲人都會厭咗愚人節嘅。

但當我玩跑跑薑餅人，到咗愚人節果陣，遊戲有個新模式，勇敢餅乾出咗個奇怪嘅挑戰營，連背景障礙物都係用MSPaint畫。果時我開始發現原來愚人節可以有咁多新意。我最鐘意嘅跑薑愚人節就係喺王國的蘑菇活動，所有餅乾的外貌都被惡搞。我真係好意外跑薑每年都會咁用心整呢啲活動。

有啲人唔係太鐘意愚人節，有好多人因為被人惡搞而受到傷害，想話番佢哋又會被人話唔識玩。因此，佢哋會覺得惡搞係個可惡嘅行為。我細個時係個敏感嘅細路，因為成日都被人講笑話，我都對於惡作劇同講笑都有啲抗拒。但玩過跑薑之後發現，惡作劇唔一定要取笑或傷害任何人。愚人節比其他節日唔一樣，有好大嘅創意空間，只要做到意想之外嘅嘢就可以整笑啲人。如果啲人惡作劇只諗到傷害人嘅話，佢哋可能本身就係唔好相處嘅人。

我上年為我啲OC頭像整咗個惡搞版，咁多個我應該最鐘意眼神死嘅Lavender。我今年雖然冇畫啲memes，但個新主題應該幾符合呢個網站嘅風格。希望你哋會鐘意啦 :33

祝你哋有個開心嘅愚人節！你哋可以係呢度見度我嘅小小創意~

P.S. 打中文真係好花腦力

I forgot how to speak Cantonese

Weather: It's warmer

Listening to: 滴草由実 - 花篝り

Mood: I don't know

Random thought: Writing is hard, but for my beloved OCs, I have to work hard òwó

April Fools is here again, I wanted to prepare something, but I was so busy with school I forgot about it. I can promise that I did draw something. Typing in Cangjie is hard.

Few days ago, I had an online interview, a website recorded me some normal interview questions. I assumed that it would use English, so I wasn't too nervous. But I realised I have to introduce myself using Chinese, which scared the hell out of me. The interview went well, but the interviewers might think I don't speak Cantonese well even though I am a HongKonger XD. I don't know if writing a blog in Cantonese on April Fools is funny, I think it's still a cool challenge.

Speaking of April Fools, do you guys actually celebrate it? I only heard of this festival when I was growing up, but I never participated in it. I'd assume people from other places would do more stuff with it, but HongKongers don't really do anything outside of the Internet. In the Internet, I often see Youtubers or other celebrities pretend to quit, only to reveal it's an April Fools joke at the end of the video or somewhere else. It's funny the first or second time, but when everyone does it, or even do it every year, it's going to get old.

But when I started playing Cookie Run, when April Fools came, there was a new game mode, where Gingerbrave got a strange trial, even the background and obstacles are drawn with MSPaint. That's when I realised April Fools can be so creative. My favourite April Fools event is the mushroom event in Kingdom, every cookie's appearances got changed. I'm so surprised Cookie Run prepares these events with so much care every year.

Some people might not enjoy April Fools, they might have gotten hurt because they got pranked, if they call those people out, they end up being seen as no fun. As a result, they see pranks as an awful act. I was a very sensitive kid, because I was constantly made fun of, I wasn't a big fan of pranks and jokes. But after playing Cookie Run, I realised jokes don't have to be at the expense of anyone. Unlike most festivals, April Fools allows a great range of creativity, just doing anything that is out of the ordinary can make people laugh. If the only joke a person can think of is hurting others, then perhaps that guy isn't very fun to hang around.

Last year I made an joke version of my OCs' profile pics, among them my favourite is Lavender who looks dead inside. I didn't do memes this time, but this new joke might match the theme of this website. I hope you guys can enjoy!

Wish you guys have a happy April Fools! You can check out OCto look at my little creative stuff~

P.S. Typing in Chinese is a pain
