Bird Watching

Watching Birds

Weather: Kinda raining

Listening to: (In my head) Attack of the Killer Queen with lyrics

Mood: Sleepy

Random thought: My nose is running so bad

Tags: Life

I don’t consider myself as a bird lover, I don’t dislike them, but I never considered keeping birds as pets unlike cats and hamsters. But one interesting thing about myself is that I sometimes enjoy taking pictures of birds on the street.

In Hong Kong, or at least the area I live, the most common birds I see are sparrows and pigeons. These birds are very common, and people usually pay no mind to these birds (unless if they are living on the air cons).

However, I have a friend who is quite fond of birds, and maybe because of that, I started to notice birds more. Moreover, I always had a habit of taking photos of interesting things I see, including flowers, pretty views, somthing like that. And eventually, birds became one of the things I would take pictures of.

I usually take pictures of round birbs, some birds look especially orbular, chonky even. Or sometimes when a bird is very close to me, I will often take the opportunity to take a pic before it flies away. Birds are often quite scaredy, when a human gets too close to them or stares at them for too long, usually they fly away. So when they unintentionally get into my camera zone, I would hurry to get my phone out, sadly they usually notice and flee before I can get a good pic.

I am no bird expert, and I don’t know all the bird species in Hong Kong yet. However, I do find birds that are not pigeons or sparrows quite cool. I like taking pictures of birds I don’t often notice, even though I have less opportunity to take a pic of them. And actually I managed to learn about a bird species, which is the red-whiskered bulbul. I usually see them hanging out on trees or bushes, so it can be difficult to get a clear picture of them. However in a morning, I saw a bulbul just chilling on a wall, and VERY close to me. And very luckily, I got a decently clear picture of it, and I got a pic again in another day!

I wonder if they are the same bird

The bird photos may have occupied some amount of space in my photo storage, but yea I like them.
