Yesterday, the long await episode 8 of TADC came out. Man so many things had happened to fandoms the past week, especially to the Cookie Run fandom. Last time from episode 7, the circus members got baited by the possibility of an exit, but got hit by the reveal that there's no exit. So now, what would they do?

Obviously, TADC EP8 spoilers

Flashback,,,

We get some lore at the very start, Caine's backstory is explained using shapes and pictures. Using the subtle hints, we learn that Caine was trained with various pictures of circuses, the C&A office etc. However, his output was not stable, therefore he was abandoned, replaced with a new AI. However, Caine managed to break out, devouring the new AI. On his own, he generated a circus, and trapped various C&A employees.

Aside from Scratch and some others, Kinger and his wife managed to be in the circus together, but eventually everyone abstracted, leaving Kinger all alone for many years. That was until Ragatha appeared in the circus that Kinger wasn't alone anymore, explaining why Kinger told Ragatha that she changed his outlook of life in ep6.

After all the drama from last episode, the members are overwhelmed, Zooble was mad at Jax for potentially ruining their only chance of leaving, while Jax debated that the button didn't matter anyway, and there could've been worse things if they did choose the blue button. But Gangle surprisingly defended Jax, saying it didn't matter what they choosed back then, and what mattered now was that they should be there for each other. And Pomni suggested that they should abandon the idea of leaving altogether, it's a tough pill to swallow, but chasing for an exit only hurts them more.

This answer is honestly so dreadful, and yet somewhat comforting. Being trapped in a circus which owner is a god who can do whatever he wants is not ideal, why would you want to do that? But this mindset is very fitting when you got hit by an event uncontrollable, sometimes there are things that can't be changed, and it is best to just focus on the things you do have control over.

I AM GOD

In Caine's office, he was lamenting over why the members were so dissatified over his adventures. Bubbles, instead of being the strange comic relief, he kept making fun of Caine that nobody likes him, and calling him the inferior of the two, callback to the beginning of the episode. Caine snapped, and went on a rant that reminded me of AM from I Have No Mouth and I Must Scream.

When he greeted the cast again, despite the members' anger, he did not tolerate any of their disagreements. Instead, he responded them with a seriousness never shown before. He then went on a musical number about how he's the one in charge, and they should not disobey him. The song is absolutely banger, and yet so uncomfortable, especially from Caine, who used to be so goofy.

Ever since that day, Caine's treatment towards the cast became worse, and it wasn't just about misunderstanding their needs anymore. The cast could not take it anymore, and it was when Pomni decided to reveal that Kinger becomes sane when he's in the darkness. When Kinger put on a bucket, he could finally answer some long awaited questions, that C&A is an artifical intelligence company, Kinger was one of Caine's creators, everyone has the ability to conjure items, and he might be able to control Caine using a console.

Apparently, this is when Jax realised that what's happening to him is real... Wait huh? He just realised that? I'm nt sure what part of the circus he thought isn't real, did he think he was in a coma, or did he think everyone except for him isn't real (which explains him calling everyone archetypes)? It gets funny if you remember Jax making fun of Pomni for thinking everything was a dream.

Kinger would need a computer to access the console, which Pomni knew where to find one: The "Exit". We then see a sequence similar to the pilot episode, where Pomni looked herself in the mirror, went through the "Exit", entering many doors and hallways, then sees an office with a suspicious computer, which she later gave to Kinger. I really love callbacks to earlier plot, and especially with the animation improvement, it shows how much the studio had gone through.

Oh no

In order to prevent Caine from stopping Kinger, the other members headed to distract Caine. But eventually Caine got bored, and grew suspicious over why the cast had been so nice to him all of a sudden.

At first I thought Caine was gonna be dumb like usual, maybe something about "Are you guys planning a birthday party for me ? 🥹" Or something funnier. But he responded with this:

Where's Kinger.

It wasn't a question, it's an interrogation, a demand. Man Caine sure has gotten smart, huh *shivers*.

Pomni got a genius idea, which is to intentionally piss off Caine by telling him how much they hated his ideas. Initially, this action was only to distract Caine, but after ten seconds it was a genuine rant on how badly Caine treated them. Caine completely snapped after that, showing them their worst fears to make them regret pissing him off. Pomni got attacked by a horror version of Gummigoo, Ragatha got abused by her mother, Gangle got reminded of her depression and got hit by a truck (which may be a suicide attempt before she entered the circus), Zooble drowned in her body parts (which represented their body dysmorphia), and Jax got peeled off by the twisted version of his friends and was made fun of (representation of his fear of showing his vulnerable side).

Blog break: Something personal

The whole part of Caine breaking down feels disturbing, not in a "Oh shit's doing down" kind of disturbing, it's a disturbance that feels similar to something in real life. Venting about my family: The way Caine switched from happy to threatening reminded me of my parents, sure they act all happy and relaxed right now, but if you DARE disagree with them just a bit, they will go "Are you complaining?" while glaring down on you. Despite them saying that they want you to trust and love them, they also have absolute authority over you, you don't have a say in things, or else you get punished. I honestly admire the circus cast for being so brave to diss Caine like that, because if I were them, I would just cower in fear and pray he doesn't hurt me.

This scene genuinely disturbed me, and yet I love it even more. I especially love Caine's voice actor voiced so good in making Caine so intimidating, and while it's strange Caine spiralled down to insanity only after Pomni arrived for a few weeks, I still enjoyed this character arc.

Oh shit

Continuing from my previous section, despite who I compared Caine to, he is less of a parental figure, and more of a neglected child. Being a sentient AI, he simply wanted to do his job properly, and yet he could never get the praise he thought he deserved. "I don't asked to be made!" Caine yelled, he never got to learn how to understand humans, and he was punished for it. He may be an AI, but he was as trapped as everyone else.

Unfortunately for Caine, he will never have the chance to learn, as his life ends here. While Kinger was on the console, he accidentally deletes Caine, possibly from accidentally hitting the delete button and Bubble sabotaging the code. Just that, Caine vanished from the circus, the circus fell apart, and the swear filter was gone.

After thoughts

For a while ago, I already thought Kinger would do something fucked up, he had been the wise figure the whole time, so maybe there would be a point he would do something unforgiving. Holy shit though, removing Caine might be one of the worst things to happen, they are still trapped in the circus, what do they do now? What do they do if they abstract? Where do they go when they get bored? What happens if they just fall in the void?

The next episode might be the last, kinda scared what happens next, but holy shit, no censors.
