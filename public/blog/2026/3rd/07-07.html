Mecca Chameleon

I suck at hide and seek

Weather: Rainy

Listening to: Yorushika - Bubble

Mood: Cranky

Random thought: I noticeably has drawn slower for this Art Fight, but it might be better since the quality has also increased

Tags: Game, Review
855 words (3 min read)

If you browse social media enough, surely you have seen a game called Mecca Chameleon, a game of hide and seek. If you used to frequent in Minecraft servers like me, the similar example is the hide and seek games where you hide as a block and camoflage within the envirnoment. Seeing this game made me feel both nostalgic and curious, as this game takes a whole new level: You have to paint your own body, as an artist myself I might have an advantage at this!

My backstory

You can skip this section if you want to hear about my review more. If I have to describe my relationship with hide and seek, it is somewhat negative. It is a fun game sure, but man am I bad at it. When playing it with my cousins, I always got caught very quickly, and I could never find any hiders. One good thing about these online games is that it takes more than hiding in a good corner to win the game, you also can find good costume or camoflage to help you blend in more. I am better at these games than the real life one, that being said, it is subjective if I am actually good at it.

Ok my actual review

When I downloaded the game, I have already watched various lets play and footages of the game, featuring different strategies and weird tactics. But the experience wasn't too exciting at first, the hunters were too dumb to find anyone, even though I was hiding in a somewhat obvious spot. That or the servers shut down before the game actually starts.

As the game finding become a bit more smooth, I went from fucking around to actually locking in. I realised one good method to hide securely is by hiding in shadows, the contours of your body isn't as visible under shadows, as opposed to hiding in bright light. Another strategy I like to use is using the blocky body type, it blends in better with flat surfaces.

Playing as the hider must be the highlight of the game, I mean why would you want to play as the boring ol shooter when you can be a little guy who can paint your body? It's really fun to find a really good play to hide and go "Man this spot is so good I bet nobody can find me!" (Then proceeds to get killed in the final one second), or maybe having to hide in a shitty spot due to indecisiveness or lack of time, but somehow surviving for a long time. I really want to win in a very ridiculous spot, like pretending to be a cactus, but man I don't have the skills or luck to pull that off!

As for playing the hunter, I wouldn't say it's not fun, there is a joy in finding hiders in secretive corners and getting the dopamine for achieving something difficult... But holy shit I'm so bad at being a hunter. In my times being the hunter, I only have one kill that I am proud of, the other times I managed to kill someone is either due to sheer luck or they are hiding in a really bad spot, and the kill count isn't high either, it's under ten. And I don't know if I'm just that bad at finding hiders, but when I go to free camera mode as a hider to find others, I could never find any, despite having a bigger angle than the hunters.

There is unfortunately another issue for me playing this game, which is motion sickness. I also experience this when playing Minecraft, so I wasn't too surprised when I felt dizzy after playing it for 30 minutes. But man, I would love to play it for longer.

Complaints

Despite my fun with the game, there are a few complaints about it. First of all, I wish it has a party mode, where a group of people can stick together and join a server without worrying them accidentally separating. Party mode is something that exists in Minecraft servers, so I hope it is also possible in this game, cuz I want to play with my boyfriend.

Second, the servers are too unstable, and I often have a hard time even play a game because the servers take a long time too start, suddenly shuts down or I kicked out when I enter, or when the game starts, the servers suddenly shuts down or the game randomly ends. I believe it might be a player-side issue than server-side issue, but I hope there is a way to ease this problem, but I do not want to spend 50% of the time in this game finding a server instead of actually playing the game.

Third, it is only available on Windows. Why are so many games only available on Windows, I wish they can make it Mac compatible as well :(

So yea, this game is mostly fun, and I recommend it. I hope this game will have more updates, it is still new so I have hopes.
