The person I have forgetten

Somewhat unnerving experience I had

Weather: Hot and humid

Listening to: Nothing at the moment

Mood: Stressed

Random thought: Man I can't find a job

Tags: Life
436 words (2 min read)

I have met many people in my life, and I usually remember someway or the other. While I may be face blind, I can vaguely remember their features in my head. Sometimes I will forget what they look like, but I would at least remember their actions, their words, and how much I can trust them. There is one person whose face I have forgotten, nor her identity, but the fact I forgotten so much about her makes me feel eerie.

It was back when I was in the final days of secondary school, and Covid 19 is still a major thing. I hadn't taken my DSE tests, and I had just taken my 2nd or 3rd dose of Covid vaccine. After class ended, I went to an public housing area and visited some sort of centre which might be for scouts. And there, I met a person, someone I knew from school, I wasn't too close with her, but I was at least on friendly terms with her. And in that centre, she gave me supplies for the Covid, some snacks, cleaning alcohol, some stuff you'd use during quarantine. She told me that I should get some rest after getting the vaccine, and after I left, I decided to visit the exam venues. And that was the last day I met the person, probably.

It isn't particularly special, you meet some acquantance, and one day you meet for the final time and you might never realise it. But after one or two years since that day, I suddenly recalled that event, and when I tried to remember who she was, I just couldn't remember it. There are many people whose face I have forgotten, but I'd at least remember their identities, like a classmate who I've taken a Luigi doll from after a bet (then promptly losing it), or a girl who me and my sister met in a park and we ran around in a princess dress, or a cram school tutor who was definitely done with our shit. But for that person, not only did I not remember what she looked like, I don't remember who she is and why did I meet her, is she a teacher? A counsellor? A mentor for a extra-curricular activity? None of these feels like the right answer, I thought for so long and so hard that my head feels dizzy, it's scary.

I always consider myself to have a good long-term memory, but I should accept that I sometimes forget details. This experience feels very scary though, it almost feels like it's supposed to be important.
