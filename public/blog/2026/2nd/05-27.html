It's been a while since my last Tomodachi Life blog, I wouldn't say much happened on the island, especially because I forgot what happened and when it happened. But there are things that I did find funny.

Lavender the professional wedding singer

For some reason, Lavender keeps being invited to be the wedding singer. It makes sense when Cosmos invited Lavender to sing, since they are good friends. But she got invited to YiXi and FengYi's wedding, Croissant and String Gummy's wedding, Solar and Timekeeper's wedding, Schneeball and Dark Fondue's wedding, AND EVEN BUN AND SHADOW MILK'S WEDDING???? Jeez she's that good of a singer?
