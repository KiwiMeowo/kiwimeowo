Since I'm graduating this year, I have been job hunting, and it hasn't been going well. You know, job market being awful and such. Because of the lack of replies from the companies I've sent my CV to, and the fact I may have messed up in one of the interview tests, I have been quite anxious. It can also be my period causing me mood swings, idk.

Speaking of my period, I have a feeling it has been causing me more pain aside from physical pain and mood swings. First of all, I'm suspecting it is one of the culprits giving me heart palpitations. While I remember random heart rate increase has always been a thing before my period started, I noticed I have been feeling more ill just before my periods*.

*I looked up my past heart palpitation incidents and period calendar, and there is not enough proof my period has any correlation with my heart palpitations.

Moreover, it seems my periods might cause insomnia. While I get sleepy at the starting day of my period, it isn't the case before that. While this effect isn't obvious before, because I've gotten less active (due to no more school), I get unwanted energy in me. It seems the hormone fluctuations made me more energetic, which is a bad thing if I want to sleep.

Enough ranting, let's talk about something more calming. While I often times despise insomnia, when I don't fight against it and do other stuff to spend the night, it is quite peaceful. It has a different vibe compared to other times in a day, it's much quieter than morning and afternoon, and a bit more... mysterious? during nighttime (I wish question comma exists).

There's one time when I couldn't sleep, I decided to watch Umamusume: The Beginning of an New Era. While the story is lacking in some parts (You can't really explain the entirety of horse racing history into 2 hours), the animation was splendid. I wouldn't call it one of my top movies, but watching it during midnight feels somewhat like a surreal experience, kinda feels like a dream.

That aside, it might not be a good idea to stay up this late, I should at least try going to sleep.
