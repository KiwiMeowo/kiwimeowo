Due to recent news, I decided to leave CRK, and the entire Cookie Run franchise fandom. This blog will be fueled by my full disappointment and anger at the recent plot development of CRK. If you enjoy the recent story, or is a fan of Shadow Milk, this blog will likely not be for your taste. It's a vent blog inconprehensibly ranting about Shadow Milk, using the dumb method of shitposting.

What's the point of following a story, if the main focus of the story is always Shadow Milk, Shadow Milk, and Shadow Milk. All characters are either overshadowed, flanderised, or overpowered by that blue clown? When he first debutted around White Lily's release, I first found him quite interesting. But ever since then, he's been appearing more and more, but his appearance is not justified, he's sometimes here, just because. Everytime he speaks, it's always some shit like "The show is getting started" or some theater themed bullshit. His appearance is more for fanservice, than serve anything good to the story.

And after White Lily died, Shadow Milk became all powerful from absorbing the other Beasts and the Ultimate Dough. And at that point, I don't even want to read what happens next in the story. But from what I've heard, it isn't any good. Elder Faerie and Silent Salt got tortured, Pitaya got turned into the Deceitling's pet, Croissant got tortured by Shaow Milk and Ash Salt, just because. And thanks to the writers, Timekeeper and the TBD was dumber than they were in CROB. Everything happening on screen feels like it's for Shadow Milk's story progression, like holy shit don't piss me off.

If the story is going to be all Shadow Milk, just call it Shadow Milk Kingdom now. I really have no reason to stay engaged in the story of CRK, and the fact CROB and CRK timelines are now connected, CROB isn't safe either. It's sad to fully leave a fandom I was in ever since 2018, but all things has to end.
