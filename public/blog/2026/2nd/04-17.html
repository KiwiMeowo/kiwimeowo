On April 15, in every country, when 12 am strikes, Tomodachi Life is released. I sat down waiting for the moment, and when the timer reaches zero, I immediately opened the long-await game...

Wow this game sure is detailed

Ok let's talk about some major improvements in this game compared to the 3DS version. First of all, the relationships are more controllable. You can just drag the Miis to characters you want to interact with, no need to rely on chance. And as for the romance system, if a Mii falls in love with someone you don't want them to fall for, you can just tell them they are overthinking.

Second of all, there's so much more you can do in the game now. Not only you need to manage the mood and levels of Miis, you also have to organise the island. As someone who isn't that great at planning buildings and stuff, it was quite difficult to clean up the place. However, it is still fun planning what building goes where.

There are also a lot of personality. In the 3DS game, you can give phrases and items to get Miis closer to the personality you want them to, but it is somewhat limited. And in this game, you can give them greeting styles, eating style, appetite, and some more.

A very big thing that is new is the custom items. Food, treasures and decoration can be customized, you can draw anything, even [CENSORED]. Like seriously, it can lead to the funniest jokes.

My Miis

After getting the game, my plan is to add the rest of my characters into the island. I had a slightest hope that I can add the Miis from other save file to my main one (e.g. the Lustrous Trio who's in a near love triangle), but it seems it's not available. So I spent hours adding all my OCs and non-OCs...

I basically added everyone here, except for Sans, Teto and the Wobbledogs. And I also added more characters outside of this picture, like the rest of the TBD, yes, everyone with a proper design. I also added Moth from Sky: COTL, I don't know what to do with them though.

Also, if you are wondering who are the personal OCs are. They are the weird imaginary friends I've mentioned before. These guys in my head figured it would be hilarious as hell if they fall in love with each other in the game, so they agreed to get a Mii design and be placed in the island. They originally don't have names, I just refer them using symbols when writing my journal, but since this game don't allow random symbols as names, I named them:

  • Q: The cynical one. I refer to her using ■, so Cube -> Qube -> Q
  • Tri: The angry one. I refer to him using ▼, so Triangle -> Tri
  • Spark: The perfectionist. I refer to her using ✦, so its Spark

Romance~

Right after opening my main save, Mira, who is in love with Lavender, asked to confess. At that point, Mira has not befriended Lavender yet, but since I've set them as couples in real life, I thought it would be fine. Unfortunately, in the middle of the confession, COSMOS APPEARED, AND LAVENDER CHOSE COSMOS INSTEAD. I enjoy this ship, but man I prefer Lavender x Mira. Anyways, Mira got depressed for a while, and it took a bit more effort to make her happy again... It took quite a while to get things right on track, Lavender fell in love with Timekeeper, Mira nearly fell for Cosmos, Cosmos nearly fell over Lavender again... But after a while, Mira managed to befriend Lavender, and later, had the confession moment. This time, Lavender accepted it, horray.

The next couple is in TBD. Coffee Candy fell in love with Baguette, and later went to confess, but Dark Fondue apparently wanted to confess as well... Baguette ended up choosing Coffee Candy in the end, wow second sapphic couple here.

The next romance is kinda messy... First, ▼ fell in love with ■. Then, Pome fell in love with ■. Also, ✦ fell in love with ▼, and ■ fell in love with Cosmos... Wow just what the hell, is this what they call a love fork or whatever? Eventually, Pome wanted to confess to ■, I knew it won't work out, but I want to see if anything funny happens. As expected, ▼ came to interrupt, and Cosmos as well?! Well, ■ and Cosmos ended up together, and I have to comfort two Miis because I thought it'd be funny to watch a love triangle- fork, or something. But at least, ✦ went to comfort ▼, they might end up together if things go well.

There's also Eternal Sugar and Hollyberry, well you can guess what happened, they are dating.

And for the long awaited, the Luminous Trio. I couldn't get Solar to fall for either of them, but somehow Claire fell in love with Jaspers first. I know I said love triangles are funny, but it gets quite sad to watch my beloved OCs become sad. And accidentally, I got Jaspers to fall for Claire as well, wow first straight ship here.

Shadow Milk was interested in Timekeeper, so I made him try confessing... while Timekeeper has a crush on Storyteller. He is depressed now, lmao.

But to do that, the Miis will need to all get along, and even if they interactions can be controlled, their reactions are still luck based. I also wanted ▼ to be friends with ■, but despite one of them used to have a crush on another, they just couldn't get along.
