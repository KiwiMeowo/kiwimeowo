Review

As happy as people are with the release of Tomodachi Life, there are several complaints about the game. While I am mostly happy about the game, and I had days of fun playing it, I do agree that there are some improvements to be done with the game.

Less conflict?

I heard that the rate of drama and conflict might be different depending on your luck, but I have yet to have Miis that fight on my island. In my 3DS save, fighting is a semi-frequent event, probably at least once a day, so I found it somewhat strange it still haven't happened here.

I'm someone who enjoys routine and peace, so I do enjoy not worrying about my fav ships divorcing. However, it might get boring in the future if there's 0 drama.

Babies don't go through aging

In the 3DS version, after a baby is born, the Miis might ask you to help taking care of them. For a few days, you get to watch the parents interacting with their baby. When the baby grows up, they become independent, and can either live outside the island or continue living on the island.

However in this game, the baby grows up immediately after the cutscene, there is no baby caring minigames. Not sure why this isn't in the game, but it is a shame indeed.
