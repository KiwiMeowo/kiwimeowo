RSS Feed

One of the best things I've added to my site is an RSS feed, allowing those with a RSS reader to follow my site. In case you don't know, RSS feed is like a notification list, if you put the link to your RSS reader, the RSS reader will notify you everytime the feed updates.

While those in Neocities can follow me to check out updates, those who aren't using Neocities will have a harder time and needs to visit my site manually to see if anything new is added. By using RSS feeds, they can see when I post art or blog posts. I've talked about implementing the RSS feeds in the blog of 27 Dec 2025, it took me a while to figure things out, but yea I did it.

Time theme toggler

I added it yesterday, it's a theme toggler, but it changes depending on what time it is now for me. If it's morning (after 6a.m. and before 10a.m.), it's morning colored, cool and dim. If it's afternoon (after 10 a.m. and before 6p.m.), it will be my default colors. If it's night (after 7p.m. and before 6a.m.), it will be dark and some images will be found glowing. If it's 6p.m. however, it will be sunset, a beautiful orange envelopes the page.

I've always thought of adding a theme toggler, but I didn't have much motivation to do it, it just felt not very special. And also I use different themes depending on the page, so a theme toggler just makes it more complicated. But theme changing depending on the time? Now that's cool. This feature will only be available on the index page for now, not sure if I will put it anywhere else though.
