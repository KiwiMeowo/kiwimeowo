Lore facts:

  • She is considered a leader type, she's quite reliable when it comes to planning stuff
  • She's very sociable, gets along with most people quickly
  • Isn't afraid to stand out, and is brave to try new things!
  • Acts a bit less energetic in hot and/or humid weathers
  • Team Spark is her team name in the event she would hold, the icon is designed by her as well

Meta facts:

  • Has a pretty short concept-art-to-final-reference time gap, normally it takes me 2 years to get from drawing their first doodle to actually making their reference, but for her it's only four months
  • I got inspired by her wing-like ponytail, that's why she likes birds
  • Team Spark is a nod to a personal OC of mine, Spark. After I absentmindedly drew Ember's design, I liked her design so much that I made Spark's design to be quite similar as well. The name fits Ember as well, with the theme of fire and stuff.
