Upon the Starry Sky (UtSS)

"Stardust Isle, the land of wizards and magic creatures thrive together. With their powers and the blessing of the gods, this magical land's peace was maintained.

Sadly the peace did not last long, this land seemed to be dying, being eaten away by a void. The people had to look for ways to keep it alive. Since then, everything has been going down hill...

Recently, a mysterious figure named Lavender emerged from the woods. Years after losing her magic abilities, a magical creature offered her new powers and gave her the mission to save the world. Will she save this island from doom, and find out the mysteries surronding this place?"
