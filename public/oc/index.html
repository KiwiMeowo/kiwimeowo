This is the place where I put all my OCs stuff, lore-heavy or silly. I am an amateur in writing stories, so please don't expect anything expertly written, there is only "fuck around and find out" ideas. Also story stuff may change without notice, and maybe story backgrounds will change 180 degrees, so who knows the characters you like might become unrecognisable the next time you check the page. In these pages, including story-writing pages, there might be hidden easter eggs, when I say hidden I mean randomly underlined text. So far there are 5 easter eggs in Upon the Starry Sky pages and 2 in Dreamland Workshop pages.

Upon the Starry Sky (UtSS)

"This is the land of wizards and magic creatures thrive together. With their powers and the blessing of the gods, this magical land's peace was maintained.

Sadly the peace did not last long, Stardust Isle seemed to be dying, being eaten away by a void. The people had to look for ways to keep it alive. Since then, everything has been going down hill...

Recently, a mysterious figure named Lavender emerged from the woods. Years after losing her magic abilities, a magical creature offered her new powers and gave her the mission to save the world. Will she save this island from doom, and find out the mysteries surronding this place?"
