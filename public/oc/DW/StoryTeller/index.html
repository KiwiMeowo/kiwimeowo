    "Without any invitation, an eccentric magician enters the land of dolls, curiously looking at the tiny workers and the universe in construction. Despite her playful personality and questionable motives, she was able to convince Charles to give her the job to manage the worldbuilding. Being a self-proclaimed show-host and story writer who seems to view life itself as a work of fiction, the dolls either keep a distance from her, or are attracted to her confidence, Does she want to stir chaos in this workplace and the Globe, or does she really want the best for the world?"

    Lore facts:

    • Despite being in a world of magic, she simply uses tricks and illusions for her colorful "magic", she says it's more interesting this way!
    • She views this world as a story, and believes there are audiences watching her and others beyond the fourth wall. However, if we go meta a bit, she never faces our POV when she tries to break the fourth wall.
    • She entering the workshop is completely accidental, but she stayed here out of curiosity
    • Her design is drawn with eyes in mind, symbolising the audience watching the show

    Meta facts:
