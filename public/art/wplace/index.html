Wplace hell yeah

Ever since I discovered Wplace, I have been quite addicted to drawing there. It is such an amazing experience to see different people's drawings and reactions. What I love the most is that I get to find people who have the same interests as me in Hong Kong, I almost thought I am the only geek in this place /j

Because the artwork can be temporary due to griefing and the work can be difficult to finish due to paint limits, I decided to make a page dedicated to my Wplace art.

(Also it would be great if you help me fill out some of the spaces. If you see empty spots with the "F" marker, you can go fill in the color. Thx :3)
