    I believe I found Will Wood's songs back in 2023 March. Through Youtube recommendations, I saw Will Wood's The Normal Album, from the vibes I thought it would be the kind I would enjoy. And at the very first song, it caught my heart. What I like the most about his songs is that they come in a wide variety of genres, Laplace's Angel is more villain sounding, Tomcat Disposables is more melancholic, and I don't know how to describe Suburbia Overture but there's screaming. My favourite song by Will Wood is Falling Up, which is very underrated. The melody sounds like childhood, wonderful song to play when you are having a breakdown (and yes I have done that I'm serious). I'm sure there's at least one song you will love in all of Will Wood's songs.
