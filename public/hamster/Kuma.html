Hammy!

Kuma looks a lot of Oreo, as they are the same hamster types. However, the most distinct feature of Kuma is that he bites everything that moves. When he first arrived, we were quite afraid of him as he would bite our hands even if we weren't trying to touch him. Now, he is less aggressive, he would still nibble when he wants us to put him back. He also loves sitting at the corner and lying his head on the bump (just like me).
