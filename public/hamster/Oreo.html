Oreo

When Oreo first arrived, he was a white and fluffy hammy, just like a cookies and cream ice cream. But as time went on, he started to grow dark fur, and he then became burnt colored. Oreo was really mild-tempered hamster, he didn't mind being pet all over. He was very emotional when it comes to cage cleaning, as he became depressed and tired everytime we cleaned the cage. A proud owner of one brain cell.
