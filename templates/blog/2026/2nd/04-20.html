The marriage

Mira and Lavender might be the first couple on the island, but the first couple to be married is Q and Cosmos! In the 3DS game, you play the proposal game by tapping when the thought bubble showing the proposer pops up. However, in this game, you play a bullet hell game, you shoot incoming bubbles to avoid distracting your mii. It sounds intimidating at first, but I noticed how easy it is to avoid them, unlike in the original game where you require 100% of your focus to not fuck up.
