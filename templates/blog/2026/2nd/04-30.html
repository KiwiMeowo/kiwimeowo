    I like taking pictures of the boiler room

    Speaking of Eva, did you know I love her so much? Did you know PJEG chapter 1 never left my mind? Like seriously, Eva is a flawed character, and yet her flaws was what made me and a lot of fans resonate with her. She represents a person who was pushed away her entire life, and she was an unfortunate victim of being isolated. Yet the plot never babied her, she did something awful, she crashed out, she was a bitter person til the very end. And that's why I love her so much, it's a realistic protrayal of what happens when someone never feels accepted, they don't act like a perfect victim, they will fight back at any possibility they might get hurt again. The story also points out the difference of toleration and full acceptance, some of the classmates might treat her nicely, but Eva knows that they weren't truly on her side, as long as they agree with Wolfgang, who is the leading person isolating her, it wasn't true acceptance. In my many years alive, watching shows where bullied characters are written like sad wet puppies, it was so refreshing and emotional seeing Eva like that, I finally felt understood after a long time, I felt I understood myself even more. What the hell, why is it a fangan that writes this kind of peak?
