It's been a while I've had a remarkable dream. While the dream is mostly non-sensical, the weirdest part has to be the emotions it gave me.

First part

For the beginning, I saw eagles in my town, and they started hunting the sparrows. As much as I feel bad about the sparrows, I was so excited to see eagles (Because I get to add them to my photo collection) and started taking photos.

After this, my aunt asked my sister if she took any photos, and was mad to hear she didnt’t. I said “Oh I did” and tried to look for them in my phone, but somehow it didn’t show up. I got so frustrated I threw it onto the floor and stomped on it, they both got scared and told me to calm down. The photos did pop up later, the photo app is just glitchy.

Second part

The next part is me wanting to join a event. It was recommended by my grandma, and took place in a kindergarten near my university, so it’s strange why I was interested. But one time I joined it and was touched by the kindness of the owner, she led me outside, chatted together and watched the neighbor cat, and I felt extremely melancholic that it would be the last time joining it.

So I joined it the second time, and it was a tag game, if a group gets tagged, the other group wins. In the end, I got tagged by the owner, and her team wins, which many people were mad since in the past hide and seeks, she always won.

When we were leaving, I was kinda sad, saying I want to take part in this event again, especially since I want to win a game of tag (I never won in this game in real life, seriously). Suddenly, the owner started singing Sugar Cloud from Riding the Cyclone, and I felt an extreme amount of joy, and everyone started singing.

Before the song was even finished, because I realised its a dream, I woke up. I’m genuinely pissed because it was an ridiculous amount of dopamine I got, why did this dream make me so happy. My current life feels numb 99% of the time, this dream makes me realise how difficult it is for me to feel joy now. What the fuck.
