In case you are a first year Art Fighter, I have a few tips:

Preparing your characters:

  • Write down any character permissions you want to set (no genderbending, no gore etc)
  • Upload your characters before AF starts, AF website gets very unstable at the beginning of the event.
  • Avoid changing character perms, refs, or hiding characters after AF starts. This is to avoid people doing an attack for your character then realising something’s outdated after they have already worked on it

Preparing to attack:

  • Don’t start your attacks before AF starts!! This is against the rules and the spirit of AF
  • Bookmark lots of characters you wanna draw
  • Just before AF starts, save the refs whose owners have already picked teams. AF website tends to crash right after it starts, you might not be able to access them through the website.
  • Check character perms and ask the owners if you are not sure about some permissions
  • If you have to take pictures of your physical art, remember to remove the metadata, it might have your address
  • Revenges are appreciated but not mandatory, if you do attacks, don’t expect for a revenge, and you are not required to revenge if someone attacks you
  • Take breaks!!!! Drawing for a long time can hurt your hands, eyes and body, you might also get burnt out. So remember to stretch, and don’t feel rushed to do attacks.
